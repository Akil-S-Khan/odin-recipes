Sheerkhurma

  1. Description

    Sheer Khurma (also known as Sheer Khorma) is a rich, decadent, traditional dessert of vermicelli pudding made during the festive occasion of Eid al-Fitr in the South Asian countries of India, Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Pakistan.

    In the Persian language ‘Sheer‘ means milk and ‘Khurma‘ means dates. This creamy and delish Sheer Khurma recipe is made with milk, dates, nuts and dry fruits, vermicelli and flavorings like rose water, saffron or cardamom powder.

  2. Ingredient

    • 1 cup sugar
    • 1 litre milk
    • 1/2 cup dry fruits chopped (almonds and pistas julienned, charoli or chironji etc.)
    • 2 tbsp ghee
    • 100 grams roasted vermicelli (crushed into small pieces)

  3. Steps

    1. Boil milk. Add sugar. Let it simmer so it thickens. Keep stirring in intervals.
    2. Heat ghee in a pan. Fry the vermicelli till golden brown.
    3. Remove from pan and let it cool.
    4. Add to the boiling milk.
    5. Garnish with dry fruits. Serve warm.
